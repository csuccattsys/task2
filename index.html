Grammar Syntax Rule: The Proximity Window

When working with correlative conjunctions like either...or and neither...nor, match your verb to the **closest subject element**.

"Neither the schedule nor the employees are changing."

Because the plural noun employees sits immediately next to the action slot, the plural verb are is required.
